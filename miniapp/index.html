🤖 EA Analyzer
⚠️ Analytical tool only. EA analysis explains trade history for educational purposes. Not financial advice. Past performance does not indicate future results.
How it works
Your MT4/MT5/cTrader EA sends trade data to our backend automatically. When a trade opens, hits SL/TP, or closes, the AI analyses the full market context — candle patterns, indicators, and timing — and tells you why the EA entered, why it won, and why it lost. Use this to fine-tune your EA's logic.

Setup
1. Download the bot for your platform
2. Open it in MetaEditor / cAlgo
3. Set UserToken = your token from /tokens
4. Set AnalysisURL = your backend URL
5. Compile and attach to your chart
6. Allow WebRequest in Tools → Options → Expert Advisors
